Puzzle Swap

3D isometric game where the player has to solve different puzzles by changing shape.

We had a team of 4 people including myself, I was in charge of programing and art.
I coded each form using scriptable objects, the ability to swap between forms, every interactable object, for example: boxes, buttons, fans, doors, etc.
In the art aspect, it was inspired by Portal, I made all the 3D assets and textures in blender, the animtions and particle effects.
